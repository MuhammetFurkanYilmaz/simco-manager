## 🎉 v1.1.21 - Purchase Builder Integration & UI Improvements

### 🚀 Improvements
* **Warehouse Inventory Integration:** The `+Reqs` popup in the Purchase Builder now displays real-time warehouse stock for required materials, complete with color-coded status indicators (Green, Yellow, Red).
* **Smart "Add Missing" Feature:** Added a new `Add Missing` button that automatically subtracts your current warehouse inventory and only adds the exact quantities you are missing to the Purchase Builder.
* **Sidebar UI/UX:** Added vertical scrolling support to the extension's sidebar, ensuring full accessibility and seamless scrolling on smaller screens or lower resolutions.
