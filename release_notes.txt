## 🌟 New Features

- **Global Headhunter (Executive Search)**: No more clicking through individual company profiles! You can now scan all talented executives in the game from a single screen. Filter by Role (COO, CFO, etc.) and Age (Youngest/Oldest) to find the perfect match instantly.
- **Realm Stats Dashboard**: Added a new live panel that shows the exact Economic Phase of your server and tracks the most and least built buildings in real-time.
- **Welcome Message**: First-time users will now be greeted with a stylish welcome screen introducing the extension's features.

## 🛠️ Improvements & Fixes

- **Quality-Specific VWAP Analysis**: The market analysis tool has been completely rebuilt. Instead of generic averages, it now calculates the average price specifically for the exact quality you are looking at.
- **Multi-Language (i18n) Fixes**: Fixed an issue causing translation placeholders to get stuck on the English interface. The extension now works flawlessly across all 12 supported languages.
- **Instant Cache Updates**: Improved the cache clearing algorithm. When the extension is updated, your local data will refresh instantly without getting stuck on old cached values.
