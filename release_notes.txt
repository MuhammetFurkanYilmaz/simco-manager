## 🎉 v1.1.19 - Financial Clarity & UI Fixes

### 🚀 Improvements
* **Financial Alerts Clarity:** Added informative tooltips (❓) to the Cashflow warnings (Net Negative, Operating Negative, Cash Drain, etc.) to help clarify exactly what each alert means and how it's calculated in real-time.

### 🐛 Bug Fixes
* **Warehouse Pricing Fix:** Resolved a critical parsing bug where items costing over ,000 (e.g., Laptops) were miscalculated in languages that use commas as decimal separators (like Turkish and German), which previously caused astronomical and incorrect profit percentage displays.
