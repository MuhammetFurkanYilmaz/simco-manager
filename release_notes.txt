### 🚀 What's New!

* **Smarter Contract Rankings:** Incoming contracts are now ranked by **Profit/Day** instead of ROI, so you can instantly spot the absolute best deals for your retail stores!
* **Personalized Profit Calculations:** Added a new **"Sales Speed Bonus (%)"** input! Just enter your HQ sales speed bonus to get perfectly accurate Profit/Day estimates.
* **Better Market Valuations:** Contract price comparisons now use true market averages (VWAP) that match the exact quality of the contract, making "Cheaper than market" alerts much more reliable. 
* **Cash Flow Protection:** Contracts that cost more than your currently available cash will now clearly display an "⚠️ Insufficient Cash" warning.
* **Light Theme Fixes:** Fixed invisible text issues for Light Theme users. (Though you might see a cheeky little reminder recommending Dark Theme! 😉)
* **Global Fixes:** Squashed bugs that caused incorrect VWAP calculations and broken text variables for players using the game in non-English languages.
* **Polished & Ready:** All 11 supported languages have been fully updated. Plus, look out for fun new support messages and a subtle version indicator in the bottom corner!
