## 🎉 v1.1.18 - The Big Contracts Update & Stability Fix

### 🚀 Incoming Contracts Rework
* **New Ranking System:** Contracts are now ranked by **Profit/Day** instead of ROI, giving you a much more accurate picture of which contracts are truly the most profitable to sell at retail.
* **Custom Sales Speed:** Added a new **"Sales Speed Bonus (%)"** input at the top of the page. Enter your total bonus from your HQ here to make the Profit/Day calculations highly accurate!
* **VWAP Integration:** Quality-based retail prices and "Cheaper than market" comparisons are now calculated using true market VWAP data rather than just the cheapest market listing.
* **Cash Warning:** Contracts that cost more than your current available cash will now display a subtle visual warning, helping you avoid accidental rejections.

### 🔧 Stability Fixes
* **Build Pipeline Resolved:** The uncommitted features from previous patches are now fully integrated and deployed. The pipeline perfectly mirrors the local Windows environment, eliminating runtime errors in Chrome caused by obfuscation inconsistencies.
